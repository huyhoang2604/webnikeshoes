Welcome to Nike Company

Nike is a multinational company from the United States that specializes in manufacturing and marketing footwear, apparel, accessories, and sports equipment. The company was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight in Beaverton, Oregon. Originally named Blue Ribbon Sports, the company was later renamed Nike in 1971.

About Us

Nike is one of the largest sports brands in the world and has a presence in almost every country globally. The company has famous brands such as Nike, Jordan, Converse, and Hurley. Nike also sponsors many famous teams and athletes in sports such as football, basketball, tennis, and running.In recent years, Nike has focused on developing sports products using advanced technology such as Flyknit, Dri-Fit, and Air Max. The company has also committed to environmental and social protection programs and has made many commitments to protecting the environment and ensuring good working conditions for workers in their supply chain.Overall, Nike's commitment to innovation, quality, and social responsibility has made it one of the most successful and recognizable brands in the world.
